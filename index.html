Journey So Far

I believe Success is a journey, not a destination. The doing is often more important than the outcome. My journey so far has been full of ups and downs. I had fascination for computer games from a very young age. Since childhood, I was very hardworking and was always curious to learn new things. My journey started when I was in 7th standard and came to know about NTSE form my friends. I was motivated by the Newspaper Ads in which the students who cracked this exam were awarded and called scholars. It fascinated me about being called a scholar. So, in 8th class I joined Iquest for preparing for NTSE. I work hard with determination and motivation and with the help of teachers and parents,I was able crack NTSE. This was the first exam that gave me a proper feel of competition at All India level. This was my first step towards my journey of life.

As we know sometimes it's the journey that teaches you a lot about your destination. After cracking NTSE, I started learning the basic concepts of maths and science of 11th and 12th for RMO and NSEJS as I enjoyed them. I represented my school in many science quizes,exhibitions at distict level and won various prizes for school. Lots of competitive exams organised by private companies were held in our school. One of them was NAO(National Astronomy Olympaid), It is an Astronomy olympaid orgainised by Orange Education. I always had an interest in knowing about stars,planets and universe. So, I filled this olympaid in order to study these things. In this olympaid I got AIR-1 and got free trip to NASA's Kenedy Space Centre.

With my hardwork and compassion, I was able to become state topper in both Maths and Science olympaids organized by SOF. I could n't crack Pre-RMO in 10th but cracked NSEJS - I was one of the three, who cracked that olympaid from Punjab. Then I started thinking about where I should take coaching for JEE-ADVANCE. So I filled admission tests of few coaching institutes. I got rank 1 TALLENTEX(admission test of ALLEN) and got cash reward and scholarship. I was also the topper of admission test of LAKSHYA. With these achievements I filled Allen Champ, which is honoring & mentoring program for rewarding the Academically Talented Students of the country organised by ALLEN and got selected for the top in country. It was my best time in life and was like a dream to me.

I decided to continue my journey with Iquest. In 11th I cracked RMO and got good score in INMO.I also cracked KVPY-I. In 12th I could n't crack any of the olympaids of physics,astronomy and chemistry. I cracked KVPY both stages and scored 304 out of 360 in JEE-MAINS and got 1599 rank in JEE-ADVANCED. I always had interest in computers, websites, mobile phones apps and things like hacking interested me lot so I took CSE in IIIT. My achievement of first year with IIIT is awesome. We were taught lots and lots of new stuff. The journey is valuable, but believing in your talents,your abilities, and your self-worth can empower you to walk down an even brighter path. I believe in hardwork and self-confidence with determination can help you achieve your dreams.
